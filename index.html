10 / FAQs
What file formats do you accept?+
We accept virtually any image format — JPG, PNG, PDF, AI, EPS, SVG, PSD, CDR, BMP, TIFF. Even low-res images can often be worked with. Just send what you have and we'll advise if we need anything else.
What embroidery file formats do you deliver?+
We deliver all major machine formats: DST, EMB, DGT, PES, JEF, VP3, XXX, EXP, HUS, VIP, SEW, and more. Just tell us your machine brand/model and we'll send the right format — at no extra cost.
How long does digitizing take?+
Standard turnaround is 3–8 hours depending on complexity. Rush orders (6hr) are available for +$5. Complex full-back jacket designs may take up to 12 hours. We'll always confirm the timeline when you submit.
Do you offer free revisions?+
Yes — unlimited free revisions until you're 100% satisfied. If the file doesn't run correctly on your machine or you need adjustments to colors, sizing, or stitch type, just let us know and we'll fix it right away.
What is the minimum order for custom patches?+
The minimum order for custom patches is 10 pieces. A free design proof is included with every order. Bulk discounts apply at 50, 100, 250, and 500+ pieces. Contact us on WhatsApp for a custom quote.
How do I send my artwork to you?+
Use the upload form on this page, email us at sales@quantumdigitizing.com, or send via WhatsApp at +92 335 233 5961. WhatsApp is the fastest — we respond within 30 minutes, 24/7.
How do I pay?+
We accept PayPal, Wise, bank transfer, and other methods. Payment is made after you approve the quote and before file delivery. For repeat clients we offer flexible arrangements. Pay per order — no subscription needed.
Do you work with international clients?+
Absolutely — we work with clients worldwide. Since we deliver digital files, there are no shipping concerns for digitizing and vector services. For physical patches and apparel, we ship internationally with competitive rates.
